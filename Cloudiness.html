Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloudiness doesn't have as much variance until you start approaching ±75° latitude. This is an example of how the weather seems to remain a consisitent cloudiness, regardless of summer or winter. Although the weather is warmer in the norhtern hemisphere at this time of year, the humidity isn't higher. Based on the humidity graph, there seems to be no correlation between cloudiness and humidity.
