Latitude vs. Humidity

The correlation between latitude and humidity isn't as apparent. The humidity level seems quite sporatic regardless of your latitude. There is, however, a slightly higher concentration of areas with 100% humidity as you get closer to the equator. This is likely due to the heat and concentration of UltraVioled rays at the equator causing bodies of water, like the ocean or lakes, to emit more water vapors.
