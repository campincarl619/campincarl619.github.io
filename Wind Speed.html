Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The average wind speed is a little higher in the northern hemisphere at the time this data was gathered. Because it is summer,it suggests that there is a correlation between the wind speed and the heat. What is strange about this, though, is that the wind speed is seldom over 10 miles an hour at the equator. If you refer back to the Max temperature graph, then there is an evident correlation between the speed of the wind and the colder temperatures. There seems to be a goldilocks zone where the wind is capable of generating speed. It is possibly due to a change in temperature, rather than just maximum temperature.
